Reasoning Transparency

A comprehensive visual guide to surfacing AI reasoning processes, decision artifacts, and processing telemetry. These concepts transform opaque AI responses into auditable, reproducible, and trustworthy interactions for power users and researchers.

CONCEPT 01

Reasoning Summary Panel

A structured 6–12 line summary that answers "Why should I trust this?" Organized by decision-relevant categories rather than step-by-step chains. Provides immediate transparency without cognitive overload.
