Your memory lives on decentralized storage. Any compatible system can access it with your permission. You're not locked into any single provider ever again.

REALITY
Your distrust is earned—the space has been full of grifters. But the underlying technology is the same cryptography that secures your bank account, your medical records, your government ID. The math is sound. The culture around it has been toxic. We're using the math.
